Musicalight

Musicalight - makes use of your phone's internal camera LED lights and microphone to create various lighting effects based on a song you are playing. 
No need for glow sticks, Musicalight is the original disco light and dance light! Just turn on any music and it takes over pulsing to the beat of the music. 
Now it's so easy to turn your home into party mode.
